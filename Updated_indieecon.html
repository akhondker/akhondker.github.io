INDIEecon

Exploring the economics of indie film!

Summary

In a world of super-sized comic book movie franchises and summer tentpole blockbusters, the niche indie film genre has declined due to reduced financing and theatrical distribution support. This site will attempt to delve into the financials behind these films and explore new film financing models.
