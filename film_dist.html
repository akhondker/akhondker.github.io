Distribution Theatrical, Digital, EST, and beyond

At the same time, new “alternative” distribution platforms (well beyond the now mainstream Hulu, Netflix, and YouTube) allow content to be distributed by content creators themselves in order to reach targeted audiences at a much lower cost than traditional channels, such as theatrical distribution. I am interested in learning how independent, micro-budget content can be financed, created, and distributed in a profitable manner through these new developments. Specifically, what crowd investing models must be employed to successfully build a financial base to support a media project, and what marketing strategies must be used with alternative distribution platforms to ensure the project is profitable and can produce a return for investors?
